# Sales script for automation audits

## Short outreach message for WhatsApp or email

Hi, I help companies automate processes like lead follow-up, collections, and repetitive operations to save time and reduce errors.

I am offering a short automation audit to identify one process that could be automated quickly. If useful, we can spend 20 minutes reviewing your workflow and I will show you what I would automate first, the likely impact, and the implementation cost.

## 20-minute discovery call structure

### 1. Opening

- Thanks for taking the time. The goal of this call is to understand one process that is currently slow or manual and see whether automation makes sense.
- If I see a clear opportunity, I will tell you what I would automate first, the expected impact, and the estimated price range.

### 2. Key questions

- What task do you repeat manually every week.
- Where do leads, follow-ups, or internal handoffs get stuck.
- What tools do you use today: Gmail, WhatsApp, Excel, CRM, forms, Stripe, and so on.
- How many people touch this process.
- How long does the workflow take today and what errors does it create.

### 4. Close

- I would recommend starting with one focused implementation.
- Setup would usually fall between `USD 800 and USD 3,000` depending on complexity.
- Typical timeline would be `5 to 14 days`.
- If that sounds right, I can send you a simple one-page proposal today.

## Common objections

### "We need to think about it"

Perfect. I will send you a one-page proposal summarizing the recommended workflow, expected impact, and next step so you can review it internally.

### "We are not sure it is worth it"

We can start with one narrow process and measure one simple outcome: time saved, faster response time, or fewer errors.

### "We cannot do something big right now"

That is fine. The idea is to start small, validate the impact, and then decide whether to expand.
